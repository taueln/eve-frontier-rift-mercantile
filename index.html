TECHNICAL OVERVIEW
How Rift Mercantile Works
Built for the 2026 EVE Frontier × Sui Hackathon. On-chain insurance infrastructure for player corporations.
The Protocol
01
POOL CREATION
A corp leader deploys a pool on Sui, deposits SUI as the reserve, and sets payout and premium amounts. The pool becomes a shared on-chain object accessible to anyone.
02
POLICY PURCHASE
A registered corp member pays the SUI premium. The contract mints a Policy NFT to their wallet — a cryptographic certificate of coverage with a timestamp anchored on-chain.
03
CLAIM & PAYOUT
After dying, the player files a claim. The dApp verifies their death against live Stillness chain data. If verified after policy purchase, the contract releases the payout automatically.
Data Sources
Kill Verification
suix_queryEvents → KillmailCreatedEvent
Queries live Stillness chain for deaths matching victim character ID after policy purchase timestamp. Fields used: victim_id.item_id, kill_timestamp.
Identity Resolution
GraphQL → PlayerProfile object
Resolves connected wallet address to EVE Frontier character ID via on-chain PlayerProfile object ownership.
Policy Records
Sui shared objects — InsurancePool + Policy NFT
All pool state and policy records are stored on-chain as Sui objects. Policies are NFTs transferred to member wallets. Claims mark policy as consumed — one claim per policy, enforced by contract.
Trust Model
Frontend-verified kills, on-chain policy enforcement
Kill verification is performed client-side against live Sui RPC. Policy records, premium payments, and payouts are fully on-chain and trustless. V2 will add oracle cosigning for fully trustless kill verification.
